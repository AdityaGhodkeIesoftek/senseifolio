About your teacher

Sumita Som

スミタ・ソム先生

A JLPT N2 certified Japanese language trainer and Japanese-English interpreter from India — teaching practical Japanese for the JLPT, JFT-Basic, and everyday communication.

Hello, はじめまして

日本語を教えて4年以上になります。

Over the past 4+ years I’ve helped 100+ students build real Japanese skills — for the JLPT, the JFT-Basic exam, and for everyday life and work. My approach is simple: patience first, pressure never.

As both a language trainer and a Japanese-English interpreter, I teach the Japanese people actually use — clear, current, and exam-ready. Classes run online over Google Meet, one-to-one or in small groups.

  • 🎓 JLPT N2 Certified
  • 🗣️ Japanese–English Interpreter
  • 📚 4+ years teaching
  • 👥 100+ students trained

Teaching philosophy

指導方針

“A language is not memorised — it is welcomed in. My job is to make Japanese feel like a friend, not a test.”

Speak from day one. You’ll say real Japanese in your very first lesson — mistakes encouraged.

Trainer & interpreter. You learn from someone who works in both languages every day, so the Japanese you learn is natural and current.

Your pace, your goals. JLPT, JFT-Basic, work, or travel — we build the path around you.
