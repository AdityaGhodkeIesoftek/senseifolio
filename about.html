About your teacher

Akebono-sensei

あけぼの先生

A certified teacher who fell in love with sharing Japanese — and decided to give it away, freely, to anyone willing to begin.

Hello, はじめまして

日本語を教えて10年になります。

I’ve spent over a decade helping learners from more than 20 countries find their voice in Japanese. My approach is simple: patience first, pressure never.

I trained in Tokyo, taught in classrooms and online, and along the way realised the biggest barrier isn’t difficulty — it’s feeling welcome enough to try. So these classes are free, forever.

  • 🎓 Japan Foundation Certified
  • 📜 JLPT N1 Examiner Experience
  • 🗼 Trained in Tokyo
  • 🌏 20+ countries taught

Teaching philosophy

指導方針

“A language is not memorised — it is welcomed in. My job is to make Japanese feel like a friend, not a test.”

Speak from day one. You’ll say real Japanese in your very first lesson — mistakes encouraged.

Culture woven in. Every grammar point comes with a story, a custom, or a song.

Your pace, your goals. Travel, anime, work, or the JLPT — we build the path around you.
