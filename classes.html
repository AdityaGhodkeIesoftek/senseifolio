Four levels, one promise: warm, structured teaching — always free. Not sure where you fit? Start with a free trial and we’ll place you.

Beginner初心者
JLPT N5

Your gentle first step — read and write kana, greet, introduce yourself, and survive your first trip to Japan.

  • Hiragana & katakana mastery
  • Core 300 words & basic kanji
  • Everyday greetings & self-introduction
  • Present/past, particles は・が・を
🖥️ Online & In-person👥 Small groups
Intermediate中級
JLPT N4–N3

Where it clicks — hold real conversations, read short articles, and handle daily life with confidence.

  • ~300 kanji & 1,500 words
  • て-form, plain form, conditionals
  • Reading short essays & menus
  • Listening to natural speech
🖥️ Online & In-person👥 Small groups
Advanced上級
JLPT N2–N1

Polish and nuance — keigo, idiom, and reading native books, news, and business material.

  • 1,000+ kanji & advanced grammar
  • Keigo (polite/humble speech)
  • Native articles, novels, news
  • Discussion, debate, presentation
🖥️ Online👥 Small groups
Conversation会話
All levels

Pure speaking practice in a friendly circle — themed each week, zero judgment, lots of laughter.

  • Real-life role-play & topics
  • Pronunciation & pitch accent
  • Slang, casual & polite registers
  • Cultural & etiquette tips
🖥️ Online & In-person👥 Open circle
