    Class FAQ

    よくあるご質問

    How much do classes cost?
    JLPT N5 is ₹12,500, N4 is ₹13,500, and N3 is ₹15,000. JFT-Basic, private classes, and group batches are priced on request — just contact for a quote.
    Do I need any materials?
    Just a notebook and curiosity. Core worksheets and the kana charts on the Resources page are shared with students.
    Online or in person?
    All classes run online over Google Meet — one-to-one or in small group batches (morning or evening).
    What if I miss a class?
    No problem — sessions repeat weekly, and notes can be shared. Just let Sumita know in advance.
    I’m a total beginner. Is that okay?
    Absolutely — beginners are very welcome. Start with JLPT N5 and we’ll guide you from あ.
