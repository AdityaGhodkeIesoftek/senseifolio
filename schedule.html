    Free class FAQ

    よくあるご質問

    Are the classes really free?
    Yes — completely. No fees, no hidden costs, no upsells. These lessons are a gift to motivated learners.
    Do I need any materials?
    Just a notebook and curiosity. All worksheets and the kana charts on our Resources page are free to download.
    Online or in person?
    Most classes run online over video; selected beginner and conversation sessions are also offered in person. Each timetable slot is labelled.
    What if I miss a class?
    No problem — sessions repeat weekly, and you’re always welcome to join the next one. Just let sensei know.
    I’m a total beginner. Is that okay?
    Absolutely — beginners are our favourite. Start with the Beginner slot or a free trial and we’ll guide you from あ.
